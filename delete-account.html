Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you would like to delete your MemoZen account and all associated data, please submit a request using the form below.

⚠️ Important Information

When you delete your account, the following data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered:

  • Your account information (email, name, profile picture)
  • All voice recordings and audio files
  • All transcriptions and AI-generated content
  • All notes, summaries, titles, and keywords
  • Subscription information (if applicable)
Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
